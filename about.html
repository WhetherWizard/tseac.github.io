What is space exploration?

In short, space exploration is exactly what it sounds like: the exploration of space. This is something that we believe will drive us as a species to continue evolving.

Why do we advocate?

We advocate for space exploration because, while humans have, in the past, gone to space through governments, the American government isn't funding the space program nearly enough. So one of two things must happen: Either, the people put pressure on the government to fund the space program more, or the people put pressure on the private sector to commercialize space exploration.

How can you join?

It's very simple. Click the blog button, go to the Quora blog, create an account, and message one of the officers, listed under Members.
